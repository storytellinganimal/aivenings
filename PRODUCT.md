# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Event organizers and designers for the AIvenings community (Hamburg-based AI meetup). They open the editor before an event, tweak colors and bubble physics to match the vibe, and export a 1080×1350 PNG for Instagram. Single-session tool — fast, focused, no accounts.

## Product Purpose

A live poster editor that recreates the AIvenings Instagram poster and lets the user customize it in real time. The poster is the output; the editor panel is a means to an end. Success = user exports a poster they're proud of in under two minutes.

## Brand Personality

Minimal · Dark · Precise. The tool should feel like a professional creative instrument — not a toy, not a SaaS onboarding flow, not a consumer design suite. The poster preview dominates; the panel quietly obeys.

## Anti-references

- **Canva / consumer design tools**: bright, playful, heavy illustrations, prominent color pops everywhere.
- **Stock Material Design 3 Android app**: stock teal/purple surface variants, large FABs, bottom navigation, rounded-everything default.
- **Generic SaaS (Notion, Linear style)**: light mode, friendly rounded cards, soft grays, whitespace-heavy.

## Design Principles

1. **The poster is the product, the panel is the tool.** Every panel decision should maximize attention on the preview, not the controls.
2. **Earn the accent.** Magenta is reserved for primary actions and active states only — never decoration.
3. **Density without noise.** Controls are tight and legible, not padded for comfort. Users are not learning the tool; they're using it.
4. **Invisible affordances.** Standard controls (sliders, buttons) behave exactly as expected. No invented interactions, no surprise.
5. **State over animation.** Motion signals state change (hover, active, loading). It does not entertain.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥ 4.5:1, large/bold text ≥ 3:1. All interactive controls keyboard-accessible with visible focus rings. Reduced-motion respected via `prefers-reduced-motion`.
